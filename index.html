Arohana 2026 – Cultural Extravaganza

JAN 3–4, 2026
Open Grounds
2,500+ Attendees

AROHANA, the Pre-Pongal celebration, marked the joyful beginning of the harvest festival with vibrant traditions and cultural spirit. The event beautifully showcased the richness of our heritage through rangoli, music, dance, and festive enthusiasm.

  • Opening ceremony with a classical Bharatanatyam performance by the Dance Society
  • Guest performance by indie artist Ananya Krishnan on Night 2
  • Inter-college ramp walk competition with 14 participating colleges
  • Best Cultural Club trophy awarded to the Fine Arts Society

Alvida 2026 – CSE Farewell event

April 1-2, 2026
College Sports Ground
800+ Participants

ALVIDA — a heartfelt farewell organized by the CSE branch, marking the end of a beautiful journey and the beginning of new dreams. It was an evening filled with emotions, laughter, memories, and gratitude, celebrating the bond between juniors and seniors.

  • Computer Science Dept. won the Overall Champions Trophy
  • Kavya R. (III Year, CSE) broke the college record in 400m sprint
  • Chess club members won gold in district-level qualifiers held the same week
  • First-ever wheelchair racing event introduced this year
